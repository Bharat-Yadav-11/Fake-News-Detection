How it works

One flow from claim to source review

FakeShield is still a review tool, not a final truth engine. The goal is to help you move from suspicion to evidence faster and with less noise.

1

Paste a clean claim

Enter one factual statement, quote, or short summary instead of multiple unrelated points.

2

Read the two verdicts

The saved model scores the wording pattern, while live lookup checks what current sources seem to support or contradict.

3

Inspect source evidence

Open the linked evidence cards, compare numbers, and confirm the publication source before trusting the claim.

Important:

FakeShield helps with rapid verification triage. It should still be backed by manual review for medical, legal, financial, or other high-stakes claims.

Trust & Security

Your data stays local. Our ML model runs on your terms.

FakeShield's hybrid architecture ensures that sensitive claims never leave your environment during local ML scoring. Live lookups are anonymized and don't store query history.

Local ML Processing

The classification model runs entirely on your infrastructure—no claim text is sent to external servers.

Anonymous Source Lookup

Live verification queries are stripped of identifying metadata before hitting external APIs.

Zero Data Retention

Queries are processed and discarded. No claim history is stored on our servers.
